About Joshfiks

Joshfiks is a Ugandan artist born July 26, 2006 in Kampala. Known for blending Afrobeat, Hip Hop, R&B and Afropop, Joshfiks released his debut single "Silence" and continues to grow as a vibrant new voice in East Africa.

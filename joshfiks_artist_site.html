About Joshfiks

Joshfiks is a 19-year-old Ugandan artist born on July 26, 2006, in Kampala. Blending genres like Afrobeats, Hip-Hop, R&B, and Afropop, he represents the next generation of African music. Known for his versatility and infectious sound, Joshfiks began his journey with the debut single "Silence", which marked the start of his rise in the East African music scene. With a unique voice and passionate style, he continues to capture hearts and create waves across the continent.
